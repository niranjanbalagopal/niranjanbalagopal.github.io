Forest Dynamics & Ecosystem Resilience

I am a wildlife biologist dedicated to understanding how landscape-scale disruptions alter ecosystem trajectories. My current research as a PhD candidate at Ashoka University (LEACHS Lab) focuses on the mid-Himalayas of Himachal Pradesh and Eastern Nepal.

I am investigating the natural succession of needle leaf and broad leaf forests, specifically how they are impacted by human-led afforestation and mono-culture plantations. I am also deeply interested in the legacy effects of wildfires—how they drive conifer-angiosperm dynamics and subsequently shift mammal behavior across these high-altitude landscapes.
