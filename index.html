Research Focus

I am a PhD Candidate working under Dr. Meghna Agarwala in the Landscape Ecology and Coupled Human Systems (LEACHS) Lab.

My doctoral research investigates ecological succession in landscapes disrupted by afforestation in the Himalayas. I combine field biology with advanced spatial analysis to understand how human interventions modify forest recovery trajectories and ecosystem services.
